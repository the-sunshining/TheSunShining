The Characteristics of a Christian Challange

The Bible says a lot about how we as Christians are to live. When I read the Bible I am always finding more characteristics of a Christian. But there is no way I could list them all on this site. So I dare you, challange you, and encourage you to read, study and meditate in your Bible to see how you as a Christian should live.

The Chracteristics

1. Christians produce fruit.

2. Christians have a relationship with God.

3. Christians are born again they are new creatures.

4. Christians love.

5. Christians live by the teachings of Jesus. - Christians are doers of the word.

6. Christians submit to the higher powers.

7. Christians share Christ. - They are a light to the world.

8. Christians are bold.

9. Christians are forgivers, they forgive.

10. Christians are hated.

11. Christians are prepared for death/judgment day, they live in light of eternity.

12. Christians use their tongue only in a way that pleases God

Help me add to the list!

The 10 Commandments

In Characteristic form

1. Christians have no other Gods before them but the one true God.

Thou shalt have no other gods before me. Exodus 20:3

2. Christians do not have idols

Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image, or any likeness of any thing that is in heaven above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth. 5 Thou shalt not bow down thyself to them, nor serve them: for I the Lord thy God am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth generation of them that hate me; 6 And shewing mercy unto thousands of them that love me, and keep my commandments. Exodus 20:4-6

3. Christians do not misuse the name of God

Thou shalt not take the name of the Lord thy God in vain; for the Lord will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name in vain. Exodus 20:7

4. Christians keep the sabbthy day holy (note it is on the 7th day)

Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy. 9 Six days shalt thou labour, and do all thy work: 10 But the seventh day is the sabbath of the Lord thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates: 11 For in six days the Lord made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day: wherefore the Lord blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it. Exodus 20:8-11

5. Christians honour and respect their parents

Honour thy father and thy mother: that thy days may be long upon the land which the Lord thy God giveth thee. Exodus 20:12

6. Christians do not kill

Thou shalt not kill. Exodus 20:13

7. Christians do not commit adultery

Thou shalt not commit adultery. Exodus 20:14

8. Christians do not steal

Thou shalt not steal. Exodus 20:15

9. Christians do not lie

Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour. Exodus 20:16

10. Christians do not covet

Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's house, thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's wife, nor his manservant, nor his maidservant, nor his ox, nor his ass, nor any thing that is thy neighbour's. Exodus 20:17
